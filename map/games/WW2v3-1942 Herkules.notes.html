A mod based on World War II v3 1942 scenario, including a new circular map with additionnal territories and reviewed sea zones, and a set of custom rules for additional (optional) players and nations:
  • French : A neutral power, with some remnants of a colonial empire and a mighty fleet
  • Commonwealth : Dominions of the British Empire, fighting alongside the Allies
  • Neutrals : Neutral and non-belligerent nations, that can join either sides under certain circumstances
